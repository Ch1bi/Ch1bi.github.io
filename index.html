Middle Man

Middle Man is a ionic app that is currently in development that lets delivery people make their delivery lists electronic instead of using paper. This makes the delivery process easier and less error prone.

Technology

Middle is built with Ionic that uses Angular for the front end, and Firebase for the backend. For tasks such as authentication, user signup, and saving business lists, Firebase will take care of that.
