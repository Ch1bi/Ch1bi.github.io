Local Shopper

Local Shopper is an Ionic app that is currently in development. It a one stop shop for all local grocery stores. It lets consumers easily choose items they would like, and place an order. As a business user, you'd be able to create your store, add items, and let a consumer know when their order is ready for pickup, and/or delivery.

Technology

Local Shopper is built with Ionic that uses Angular for the front end, and Firebase for the backend.
