Process

My process for developing this landing page was straightforward, however I did learn a few things as far as interacting with the client, and working on an exisiting codebase.

01. Finding the client

I initially found the client online through a forum. They needed help with the frontend of their site since they were primarily a backend developer. Before anything, the client wanted to know if I knew React, see examples of my work, and my github. Once I showed these items, we decided to schedule a video call.

02. Client's Request

During the video call, the client explained that they wanted a revamp of their homepage, with a few ideas. After the video call I got to work doing some research and began to look through the code.

03. Getting started

Initially when I looked through the code, I felt overwhelmed. The client's project was surprisingly well developed with React + Redux. I took a moment to relax then began to look through the code to get a feel where everything is, and to get a sense of their coding style. If I didn't know where or how a particular component connected to one another, I would ask the client a question, and they explained it to me.
